## About Me

I am a 3rd year PhD student in the Mathematics Department at Imperial College London, supervised by [Dr Nikolas Kantas](http://wwwf.imperial.ac.uk/~nkantas/). I am jointly funded by the EPSRC [Centre for Doctoral Training in the Mathematics of Planet Earth](http://mpecdt.org/) and the [National Physical Laboratory](https://www.npl.co.uk/). I hold an MRes in Mathematics and an MSc in Statistics from Imperial College London, and an MA in Mathematics from the University of Cambridge. 


## Research Interests

My research focuses on the development and analysis of efficient computational methods for inference, learning, and optimisation in complex stochastic systems. I am particularly interested in the application of these methods to high-dimensional state space models arising in environmental monitoring applications.
